# Executive Copilot Demo (Concept)

## Problem
Executive support work often includes repetitive coordination tasks that consume time:
- Meeting brief preparation
- Calendar conflict triage
- Stakeholder update drafting
- Travel contingency planning

## Concept
Executive Copilot uses reusable AI prompt workflows to accelerate those tasks.

---

## Demo Workflow Example

Input:
Raw meeting notes + agenda + stakeholder list

AI Workflow:
1. Summarize notes
2. Surface decisions needed
3. Flag risks
4. Draft action tracker

Output:
Executive briefing packet generated in minutes.
